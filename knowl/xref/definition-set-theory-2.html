Definition 1.4.28.

The union of two sets \(A\) and \(B\text{,}\) denoted by \(A\cap B\text{,}\) is the set of all elements that are in \(A \text{ and } B.\) That is,
\begin{equation*} A\cap B=\left\{ x:\left ( x\in A \right ) \wedge \left ( x\in B \right )\right\} \end{equation*}
